One app fits all

By developing your app on our open-source SDK, you are guaranteed that one app can go cross operator devices. No multiple app iterations required to work on different operator networks and tiers of devices.

Monetize your content

Start generating revenues on selective operator devices through in-app operator billing, advertisement and analytics.

Insert advertisements

With the AdSDK, (targeted) advertising campaigns can be published to millions of households. The AdSDK makes ad campaigns simple and effective, featuring with pre- mid- post- rolls for ad agencies and publishers.

Offer paid content

Our payment API enables simple conversion for tVoD- and sVoD-based Apps, plugging into the operator billing platform. Difficult registration procedures are not needed.

Analytics

Our Dashboard provides real-time insight into big data, which app providers can use to analyse and optimize the app experience and drive revenue opportunities.
