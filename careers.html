Careers

We are Metrological, and since 2006 we have been building an innovative TV Platform that TV Operators can use to give their customers access to the latest TV apps and OTT content.

Metrological is a market leader in the industry, and our platform is now live on 3 continents, with a reach of 40 million households. Our clients include well-known brands, such as Comcast and Liberty Global. Our reach is expanding rapidly, and we are looking for talented and creative colleagues who want to grow along with us.

We are a dedicated team of developers, engineers, designers, business minds, and legal experts. From our offices in Rotterdam and Silicon Valley, every day we work on innovations in the field of technology and entertainment. Our platform and expertise is trusted by some of the biggest international media brands like Netflix.

We have an open culture; everyone is approachable, and we can talk about anything. We trust our employees, and we offer a flexible work environment with flexible hours. Plus, we offer everyone plenty of space to develop and grow professionally.

We have done some pretty impressive work that wouldn’t have been possible without our brilliant team of people. And now, you could be one of them. Are you curious, creative, results-driven, and most importantly, would you like working with us? If so, then we would love to hear from you!

If you have any questions, or can’t find the job opening you’re looking for, then please don’t hesitate to contact us, because we are always interested and welcome open applications.
