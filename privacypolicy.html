Privacy Policy

Metrological refers to Metrological Group NV and its affiliates, having its principal place of business at 3011NK Rotterdam, Mariniersweg 151, The Netherlands. This privacy policy describes how your personal data is collected, processed and protected when i) you have showed interest in our products or services in order to receive marketing information and, ii) you use the Metrological Dashboard which we offer to Content Providers and App Store Operators.

Personal data

In this privacy statement personal data means any information relating to a natural person that directly or indirectly can identify that natural person.

Why and how we process your data

Marketing:
  • We send marketing emails about our products and services to our existing customers whose contact details we have obtained due to our customer relationship with them.
  • We send marketing emails to persons who have expressed an interest in Metrological’s products or services.
Dashboard
  • Metrological processes your data in order to make sure that you will be able to properly use the dashboard functionality in order to enable the Content Provider and the app Store operator to gain insight on the usage of the App and the App Store. This functionality is made available via https://dashboard.metrological.com.

Types of data we collect

For the purposes set out in this privacy policy, we collect and process the following data that we have on you:

Marketing:
  • First name and surname
  • Email address
  • Name of the company that you represent
Dashboard
  • Username and password
  • Email address
  • User settings

Lawful basis for processing your data

For the marketing purposes, we rely on the fact that you have shown interest in Metrological’s business and requested for updates. You can unsubscribe from receiving our marketing emails by clicking on the unsubscribe link provided in every email. Please note that if you do unsubscribe, we will not be able to inform you anymore about our products, services and activities.

Within the Dashboard, we collect and process your data on the ground of the necessity for the performance of our agreement. Since we have concluded agreements with both Content Providers and App Store Operators in which we have stated that a tool will be provided which gives an insight on the usage of the App and/or App store. Therefore, in this particular situation it is deemed necessary to process such data.

Email service provider

We use MailChimp to manage our e-mail communications. Their privacy policy is available at http://mailchimp.com/legal/privacy.

Retention

We keep your personal data for marketing purposes for as long as it is deemed to be necessary to fulfill our purposes. However, if you decide to opt-out, we will no longer use your personal data for this purpose. If the data is not needed for some other purpose like for example for our customer relationship, we will immediately delete the data.

The storage of your personal data within the Dashboard is linked to the term of the applicable concluded agreement. After termination or expiration of this agreement, Metrological will use best efforts to make sure that the data will be deleted immediately.

Security

Metrological takes the security of your personal data very seriously. In order to prevent unauthorized access or disclosure, Metrological has put in place appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard and secure your personal data. If you would like to have more information about how we store and protect your data, please contact us directly.

Your rights over the personal data we hold

In accordance with applicable rules, you can reasonably request access to the personal data we hold on you, at any time. In addition, you may request that inaccurate personal data held on you to be rectified or completed or for your personal data to be erased.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

Metrological reserves the right to change this privacy policy in order to comply applicable laws and regulations
