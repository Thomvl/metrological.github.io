Cookies

We use cookies to make our website function properly and to analyse our traffic. We also share information about your use of our site with our analytics partner who may combine it with other information that you’ve provided to them or that they’ve collected from your use of their services.

Cookies are small text files that can be used by websites to make a user's experience more efficient.

The law states that we can store cookies on your device if they are strictly necessary for the operation of this site. For all other types of cookies, we need your permission.  This site uses different types of cookies. See below for the list of the cookies used on our website.   You can at any time change or withdraw your consent from the Cookie Policy on our website.

Necessary

Necessary cookies help make a website usable by enabling basic functions like page navigation and access to secure areas of the website. The website cannot function properly without these cookie

Statistics

Statistic cookies help website owners to understand how visitors interact with websites by collecting and reporting information anonymously.

Unclassified

Due to how the internet and websites work, it is possible that third parties place cookies via the domains without us knowing about it.

If you encounter cookies in our domains that fall into one of the above categories and are not mentioned in this explanation, please let us know. Or contact the third party directly and ask which cookies they put, what the reason is, what the lifespan of the cookie is and how they have guaranteed your privacy.
